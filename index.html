Interesting Facts about Camel

Chameleon

Interesting Facts about Chameleon

  1. Most chameleons change their body color based on their surroundings to hide from predators
  2. Chameleons are reptiles that are part of the iguana family
  3. Changing skin color is an important part of communication among chameleons
